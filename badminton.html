Essential Gear

To play badminton, you'll need the following items::::::::::::::::::::::::::::

  • Racket: Choose a lightweight racket for better control.
  • Shuttlecock: Feather or nylon, depending on your preference.
  • Shoes: Non-marking shoes for court play.
  • Clothing: Breathable, lightweight sportswear.

Basic Techniques

Improve your game with these essential techniques:

  1. Grip: Hold the racket properly for forehand and backhand shots.
  2. Footwork: Position yourself effectively for every shot.
  3. Serve: Learn high serve, low serve, and flick serve.
  4. Smash: Deliver powerful, angled shots.

Basic Rules

Key rules every player should know:

  • A match consists of the best of 3 games of 21 points.
  • A player scores a point when the opponent fails to return the shuttlecock within the boundaries.
  • The shuttlecock must be hit below the waist during a serve.
  • The game begins with a toss to decide serve or side.
